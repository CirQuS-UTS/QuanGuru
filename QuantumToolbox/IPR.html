iprKet(basis: List[Union[scipy.sparse.base.spmatrix, numpy.ndarray]], ket: Union[scipy.sparse.base.spmatrix, numpy.ndarray]) → float[source]¶

Calculates inverse participation ratio of a ket in a given basis . The complex probability amplitudes satisfy , therefore IPR = 1 is perfectly localised, and IPR = is uniformly localised in dimensional space.

Parameters

  • basis (matrixList) – a ket state

  • ket (Matrix) – a complete basis

Returns

inverse participation ratio

Return type

float

Examples

>>> completeBasis = completeBasis(dimension=2)
>>> state0 = normalise(0.2*basis(2, 0) + 0.8*basis(2,1))
>>> iprKet(completeBasis, state0)
1.1245136186770428
>>> state1 = normalise(0.5*basis(2, 0) + 0.5*basis(2,1))
>>> iprKet(completeBasis, state1)
2.000000000000001
>>> state2 = basis(2,1)
>>> iprKet(completeBasis, state2)
1.0
